Prizes

1. Early Bird Prizes

The first student to enter his/her data for each test day will get a small science related prize. These will be awarded to both schools, announced and delivered on Friday.

Week 1

Three students from Northpoint and three students from Discovery were the science prize winner for being the first students to enter data on the website for the water tests on each testing day! The prize for each of them is a cloud identification wheel.

Week 2

Three students from Northpoint and three students from Discovery were the science prize winner for being the first students to enter data on the website for the water tests on each testing day! The prize for each of them is a science journal to keep notes, observations and ideas.

2. Science Prize Draw for participating students
