Real-Time Data Collection from Twitter

This application allows you to collect tweets from Twitter in real-time about events of your interests using our developed API. You can specificy several key words and move a locator to any place around the world to filter the tweets that match your criteria.













Timeline Recovery of Real-World Events

This application takes millions of tweets created during an event and constructs the story of the event by picking out the most important tweet(s) during the time interval specified by the user.
